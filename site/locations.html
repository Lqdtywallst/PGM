Coverage Notes

Built around how guests actually move through Dubai

The best handover point depends on luggage, hotel access, arrival timing and how much visibility or convenience matters for the trip.

  • Palm and Marina work well for resort stays and waterfront hotels.
  • Downtown is strongest for central business and event schedules.
  • Airport handovers need prior booking and cleaner timing coordination.
  • JBR remains a practical beach-stay option with fast city access.

Handover Process

How location planning works

The handover becomes much smoother when the team has the stay, timing and model locked in early enough to coordinate the right route.

1. Pick the car

Start from the fleet page or go straight to reservation if you already know the model.

2. Confirm the zone

Share the hotel, residence or airport details so the team can line up the most practical handover point.

3. Align timing

Arrival windows, building access and luggage needs matter more than a simple map pin in premium handovers.

4. Receive one-team support

The same team handles confirmation, documents, delivery and return planning so the experience stays consistent.

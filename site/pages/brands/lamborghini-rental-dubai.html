What affects Lamborghini rental prices?

  • Season & demand - peak tourism windows and major events in Dubai can tighten availability.
  • Rental length - multi-day and longer hires often improve value versus a single peak-day booking.
  • Model & mileage - supercars and high-performance SUVs may include different mileage bundles and security deposit rules.
  • Delivery zone & timing - airport, Palm and late-night handovers may involve supplements (quoted in advance).

Requirements to rent a Lamborghini in Dubai

Visitors

Typically: valid passport, visit visa if applicable, home-country driving licence and, for many nationalities, an International Driving Permit. Minimum age requirements apply per vehicle category.

UAE residents

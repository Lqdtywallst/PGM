Service Standards

What every premium booking should feel like

The better competitor pages are very clear about process. We do not want to become generic, but we do want the guest to understand what “premium service” means in practice.

Clarity

Clear confirmation before handover

Guests should know the model, the delivery window and the next action instead of chasing updates across multiple messages.

Coordination

One-team delivery planning

The same team should manage documents, location planning, arrival timing and return notes so the booking feels consistent.

Fit

The right car for the right stay

Guests should not be pushed to the flashiest option if the trip really needs luggage room, easy access or a calmer daily rhythm.

Support

Fast route to a real answer

WhatsApp, phone and reservation should all lead to the same booking outcome, not three disconnected conversations.

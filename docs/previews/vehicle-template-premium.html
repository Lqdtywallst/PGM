FAQ

The same recurring questions should be answered cleanly.

Why is the title below the gallery instead of above it?
Because the first screen needs to feel like a premium product page first and a copy block second.
Why is the video not inside the right rail?
Because the top gallery should read as one composed still-led block. Video belongs lower, where movement can add context without fragmenting the first impression.
What changes from car to car once the template is approved?
Media, narrative, use cases, pricing logic and operational notes. The page order and the visual rhythm stay fixed.
What has to be locked for SEO before a real model page goes live?
Unique metadata, canonical, structured data, descriptive alt text, real internal links and copy that is specific to that exact vehicle and booking intent.

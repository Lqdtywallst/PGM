# Premium Copy Guide

Dynasty Prestige copy should feel calm, precise and operationally confident. The tone is luxury without noise: clear booking steps, discreet support, careful handover language and no exaggerated guarantees.

Use:
- Premium, concrete language: car, route, dates, handover, hotel, villa, airport, chauffeur, WhatsApp, secure checkout.
- Confidence through process: explain what happens before payment, after 50% upfront and before delivery.
- Clear CTAs: `Reserve`, `Start reservation`, `WhatsApp the team`, `Call`, `Email`, `View fleet`, `Find booking`.
- Safe promises: say delivery and availability are coordinated or confirmed, not guaranteed unless the business has explicitly verified it.
- Client-facing errors that give the next step: retry, WhatsApp support or check reservation details.

Avoid:
- Technical language in client copy: backend, server logs, PaymentIntent, client secret, stack trace, localhost.
- Empty luxury claims: best, ultimate, guaranteed, VIP everything, flawless without proof.
- Mixed Spanish/English in public surfaces.
- Mojibake, placeholders, `N/A`, TODO copy and generic CTAs like `More information` when a clearer label fits.
